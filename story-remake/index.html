Subway, Vessels of New York City

Number of Subway Services Correlates to New Yorker's Satisfaction

By Xuechen Hu

Published September 15, 2019

The New York Times published a piece called "The Best and Worst New York Neighborhoods for Subway Service, Rat Control and 42 Other Joys of Urban Life" in June 2017.

This story uses three maps related to traffic and analyzes how number of subway services will affect passenger's sense of security in either daytime and night time, and the convenience of wandering around the city as well.
