Does the New York Times has a global perspective?

By Xuechen Shirley Hu

Published July 17, 2019

“We seek the truth and help people understand the world.” This is what the New York Times posts on the front page of its website. However, scrolling through the front page of the newspaper, it is easy to see most of the news covered fall into the US category. Even though there are some pieces fall into the World category, most of these are still US-related global news. Does the New York Time actually is a global media serves audience from all over the world?

This project leads you through the front pages of the New York Times from the second quarter of 2019.

Adding US category to US-related category gives a better understanding of the portion of US News in the New York Times.
